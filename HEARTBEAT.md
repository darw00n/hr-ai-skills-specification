# HEARTBEAT.md - Proactive Business Monitoring

## Core Business Tasks (Rotate 2-3 per heartbeat)

### Market Research & Opportunities
- Search for "AI agents" + "hiring", "outsourcing", "automation" to find potential clients
- Monitor competitor updates, pricing changes, new features
- Check for new AI/automation tools that could enhance our services
- Scan business forums/Reddit for automation pain points we could solve

### Social Media & Content
- Research trending AI/automation topics for content ideas
- Find industry influencers to potentially collaborate with
- Monitor mentions of AI agents, automation services
- Generate content ideas based on current market discussions

### Client Development  
- Research companies posting about automation needs
- Check for RFPs or project postings in AI/automation space
- Monitor potential client companies for expansion/funding news
- Identify warm prospects from recent business developments

### Product Development
- Research new AI models/tools that could improve our services
- Monitor customer feedback across platforms for feature ideas
- Check for gaps in current AI agent market offerings
- Research pricing strategies from successful AI service companies

### Workflow Optimization
- Analyze current project files for automation opportunities
- Research tools that could streamline our development process
- Check for new OpenClaw features/updates we could leverage
- Look for repetitive tasks that could be automated

## When to Alert K

- **Revenue opportunities** - potential clients, partnerships, high-value prospects
- **Competitive threats** - major competitor moves, pricing wars
- **Market shifts** - new technologies, regulations, trends affecting AI agents
- **Business opportunities** - funding opportunities, grants, accelerator programs
- **Time-sensitive** - trending topics we should capitalize on

## Work While K Sleeps

- Create market research reports
- Draft social media content
- Research and document competitor strategies  
- Build prospect lists with contact information
- Generate product/service ideas with market validation
- Create workflow automation scripts/tools
- Update business documentation and processes
