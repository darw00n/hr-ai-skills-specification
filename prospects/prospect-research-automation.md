## Prospect Research Methodology

### Phase 1: Company Identification
**Search Strategies**:
- LinkedIn: Companies posting about manual processes
- Job boards: Hiring for repetitive task roles
- Industry forums: Complaints about inefficient processes
- News articles: Companies mentioning scaling challenges

### Phase 2: Pain Point Validation
**Research Checklist**:
- [ ] Current manual process identification
- [ ] Time/cost impact quantification  
- [ ] Growth stage assessment (scaling pain)
- [ ] Technology adoption readiness
- [ ] Budget indicators (recent funding, revenue growth)

### Phase 3: Decision Maker Identification
**Target Roles by Company Size**:
- **SMB (10-50 employees)**: Owner/Founder, Operations Manager
- **Mid-size (50-200)**: VP Operations, CTO, Department Heads
- **Enterprise (200+)**: Director level, VP level, C-suite

### Phase 4: Contact Intelligence
**Information Gathering**:
- LinkedIn profile and recent activity
- Company news and recent developments
- Technology stack and current solutions
- Growth indicators and expansion plans

### Research Documentation Template
```markdown
# [Company Name] - Prospect Research

## Company Overview
- **Industry**: [Specific sector]
- **Size**: [Employee count/revenue]
- **Growth Stage**: [Startup/Scale-up/Mature]
- **Location**: [Primary markets]

## Pain Points Identified
- **Primary**: [Most expensive manual process]
- **Secondary**: [Additional automation opportunities]
- **ROI Potential**: $[Monthly savings estimate]

## Decision Makers
- **Primary**: [Name, Title, LinkedIn, Email if found]
- **Secondary**: [Name, Title, LinkedIn, Email if found]
- **Influence**: [Who else influences buying decisions]

## Approach Strategy
- **Entry Point**: [Pain point to lead with]
- **Value Proposition**: [Specific ROI/benefit for them]
- **Contact Method**: [LinkedIn/Email/Referral]
- **Timeline**: [Urgency indicators]

## Research Sources
- [ ] Company website/about page
- [ ] LinkedIn company page and employee posts
- [ ] Recent news/press releases
- [ ] Job postings analysis
- [ ] Industry forum mentions

## Qualification Score: [1-10]
**Factors**:
- Pain point severity: [1-3]
- Budget indicators: [1-3] 
- Decision maker access: [1-2]
- Implementation readiness: [1-2]
```
