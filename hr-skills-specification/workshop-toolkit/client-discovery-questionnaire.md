### 1. RECRUITMENT & ACQUISITION (18 skills)

#### Current State Assessment
- **Recruitment Volume**: How many hires per month/quarter?
- **Time-to-Fill**: Average days from job posting to offer acceptance?
- **Quality Metrics**: How do you measure hiring success and retention?
- **Stakeholder Involvement**: Who participates in hiring decisions?
- **Technology Stack**: What recruiting tools are currently used?

#### Automation Opportunities
```yaml
high_priority_candidates:
  - "Job posting creation and distribution"
  - "Resume screening and candidate matching"
  - "Interview scheduling coordination"
  - "Offer generation and approval workflows"

configuration_requirements:
  - "Company-specific job templates and branding"
  - "Multi-level approval processes for hiring"
  - "Integration with external job boards and ATS"
  - "Custom screening criteria and knockout questions"
```

#### Success Criteria
- **Efficiency Gains**: Target reduction in recruiter administrative time?
- **Quality Improvements**: Better candidate-job matching accuracy goals?
- **Experience Enhancement**: Candidate and hiring manager satisfaction targets?

- **Stakeholders**: Who's involved in onboarding (HR, IT, Facilities, Managers)?

- **Variations**: Different processes for roles, locations, or levels?
