# Social Media Strategy - AI Agents Business

## Platform Strategy

### LinkedIn (Priority #1)
- **Target**: Business decision makers needing automation
- **Content**: Case studies, industry insights, automation tips
- **Frequency**: 3-5 posts per week
- **Goal**: Establish K as AI automation expert

### Twitter/X (Priority #2)  
- **Target**: Tech community, AI enthusiasts, entrepreneurs
- **Content**: Quick tips, industry news commentary, behind-the-scenes
- **Frequency**: Daily tweets + engagement
- **Goal**: Build thought leadership and network

### YouTube (Long-term)
- **Content**: Tutorials, case studies, AI agent demos
- **Goal**: SEO traffic and lead generation

## Content Pillars

### Educational Content (40%)
- How AI agents solve business problems
- Automation best practices
- ROI case studies
- Industry trend analysis

### Behind-the-Scenes (30%)
- Development process insights  
- Client success stories (anonymized)
- Tool reviews and comparisons
- Lessons learned

### Engagement Content (20%)
- Industry polls and questions
- Controversial takes on AI/automation  
- Community discussions
- Trend commentary

### Promotional Content (10%)
- Service announcements
- Client testimonials
- Special offers
- Portfolio highlights

## Content Calendar Framework

### Weekly Schedule
- **Monday**: Industry insight/trend analysis
- **Tuesday**: Educational content (how-to)
- **Wednesday**: Behind-the-scenes/case study  
- **Thursday**: Engagement/community content
- **Friday**: Week recap/lessons learned

### Content Batch Creation
- Research trending topics weekly
- Create content templates
- Schedule posts in advance
- Track engagement and adjust
