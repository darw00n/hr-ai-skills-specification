## Research Checklist

### Before Contacting
- [ ] Confirmed they have the identified pain point
- [ ] Found decision maker contact information
- [ ] Researched recent company news/growth
- [ ] Identified specific ROI opportunity
- [ ] Crafted personalized outreach message

### Quick Qualification Questions
1. Do they currently do this process manually?
2. How many hours/week does it take them?
3. What's their current annual revenue? (determines budget)
4. Are they growing/scaling? (creates urgency)
5. Do they use other automation tools? (shows openness)

**Goal**: Use this template to systematically research and qualify 10+ prospects daily
